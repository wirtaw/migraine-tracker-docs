# 🏠 Welcome to Migraine Tracker

Living with chronic migraines can feel unpredictable. **Migraine Tracker** is an open-source platform built to help users reclaim their peace of mind by finding hidden patterns between their daily lives, weather shifts, and solar activity.

This documentation serves as the central hub for everything related to the Migraine Tracker ecosystem.

### What is Migraine Tracker?
At its core, Migraine Tracker is a data-correlation engine. It allows users to securely log their daily health metrics and specific migraine incidents. The platform then correlates this personal data with global environmental telemetry (like barometric pressure, UV index, and solar flux) to gently predict and warn users of potential upcoming risks.

### Who is this documentation for?
We have designed this guide to serve multiple audiences:
* 🌱 **Users:** Looking to understand how to read their dashboard or how their sensitive health data is protected.
* 🖥️ **Frontend Developers:** Looking to contribute to our React/Vite dashboard and build beautiful, accessible UI widgets.
* ⚙️ **Backend Developers:** Looking to integrate new environmental APIs or optimize our NestJS prediction engine.
