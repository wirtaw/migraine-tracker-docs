# 🏡 Your Daily Dashboard

Your Dashboard (`/dashboard`) is designed to be your serene daily command center. We've organized everything so you can get a quick glance at your health and your environment without feeling overwhelmed. 

Here is a quick tour of what you will see:

## ⚡ Quick Add Buttons
At the very top, you'll find your **Tracking Buttons**. These are the most important tools in the app! Use them to quickly log a daily **Health Metric** (like sleep or water intake), record an **Incident**, or check off your daily **Medications**.

## 📅 The Calendar View
A gentle, visual overview of your month. Days where you logged a migraine or a health entry are subtly highlighted, allowing you to see your consistency and incident frequency at a single glance.

## 🌍 Environmental Widgets
* **Weather Widget:** Displays today's temperature, conditions, and crucial barometric pressure shifts.
* **Solar Widget:** A unique look at invisible environmental factors, including the current UV Index, Solar Flux, and Geo-Magnetic K-Index.

## 🔮 Daily Risk Widget
Powered by our *Pattern Guardian*, this widget takes your historical logs and today's weather/solar data to give you a daily percentage risk. If the risk is high, it's a gentle reminder to hydrate and keep your rescue medications close!

## 📊 Life Stats & Biorhythms
* **Life Metrics:** A quick summary of your recent sleep quality, hydration, and overall mood.
* **Biorhythms:** A beautiful chart mapping your natural Physical, Emotional, and Intellectual energy cycles for the current day. 

## 🕰️ Most Recent Incidents
A short list of your latest logs, helping you remember when your last migraine occurred and how severe it was without having to dig through your history.
