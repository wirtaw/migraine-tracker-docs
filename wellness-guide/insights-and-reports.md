# 📈 Insights & Reports

While the Dashboard is perfect for your day-to-day check-ins, the **Report Page** (`/reports`) is where you step back and look at the bigger picture.

We designed this page to help you and your healthcare provider spot long-term patterns.

## 📅 The 30-Day View
The Report Page defaults to a beautifully charted view of your **Last 30 Days**. 
* You can see exactly how many incidents you had this month compared to the previous month.
* It visually graphs your pain severity over time, showing you if your migraines are generally getting milder or more intense.

## 📊 Statistics by Incidents
Instead of just showing raw data, we break down your incidents into actionable statistics:
* **Most Frequent Symptoms:** See a visual breakdown of what symptoms accompany your pain the most (e.g., 80% of your migraines involve Light Sensitivity).
* **Most Frequent Triggers:** Discover which of your custom triggers are popping up most often right before an incident.
* **Medication Efficacy:** Review which rescue medications you relied on most heavily over the last month.

## Sharing with your Doctor
Because this data is neatly organized into visual charts, the Report Page is an excellent tool to bring to your neurologist or general practitioner. Instead of trying to remember how many headaches you had last month, you can simply show them your 30-Day trend!
