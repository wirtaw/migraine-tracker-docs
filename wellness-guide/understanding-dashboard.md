# 📊 Understanding Your Dashboard

When you log into **[Migraine Tracker](https://migrane-tracker-dashboard.sliplane.app)**, your dashboard serves as your daily command center. It connects the dots between how you feel internally and what is happening in the world externally.

Here is a quick guide to what you are looking at!

## 🌤️ The Environmental Widgets
Sometimes, your triggers are completely out of your control. We automatically fetch localized data to help you spot hidden environmental patterns.

* **Weather & Barometric Pressure:** Rapid drops or spikes in barometric pressure are common migraine triggers. We chart this alongside your incident logs to see if your body is sensitive to incoming storms.
* **Solar Radiation (UV Index & Solar Flux):** It sounds like science fiction, but shifts in solar activity and geomagnetic storms (K-Index) can affect human biorhythms and trigger headaches in sensitive individuals. We track these invisible metrics for you!

## 🧬 Your Biorhythm Chart
Your body runs on natural cycles. Based on your birthdate, we calculate your physical, emotional, and intellectual energy levels.
* Have you noticed that your migraines hit when your physical cycle is at its lowest? 
* This chart helps you visualize your energy reserves so you know when to rest and when to push forward.

## 💊 Medication Tracker
If you rely on daily preventatives or rescue medications, this widget keeps your routines organized. It helps you see at a glance if a missed dose earlier in the week correlates with a migraine today.

## 🔮 The Risk Forecast
As you log more days, this is where the magic happens. Our `Pattern Guardian` engine quietly analyzes your history and compares it to today's weather and solar data. If it spots a familiar pattern that usually leads to a migraine for you, the Risk Forecast widget will gently alert you, giving you time to prepare, hydrate, or take preventative measures.
