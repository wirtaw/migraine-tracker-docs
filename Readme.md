## 🛡️ Our Commitment to Privacy
Because we deal with highly sensitive medical logs, privacy isn't just a feature—it is our architectural foundation. Your data is encrypted at the application layer using dynamically rotating keys, and you have total control to export or permanently erase your history at any time. Read more in our **[Privacy Promise](wellness-guide/privacy-and-data.md)**.
